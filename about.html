Program Goal

The goal of the program is to offer an accelerated and demanding curriculum for our most gifted and talented students who are considering attending highly competitive colleges and universities. The Program will also give the students exposure to many different fields, using a variety of educational expediencies, to find their perfect niche. This will eventually lead to the students not walking into their higher education wondering what to study.
